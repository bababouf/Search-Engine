Overview

Our web application offers a comprehensive solution for efficient search and indexing, empowering users to explore vast corpora with ease and precision. Whether utilizing our default corpus or uploading their own, users experience seamless access to relevant information through a streamlined interface. Below are the main features of this application:

Corpus Selection

Users have the flexibility to choose between our default corpus or upload their own, catering to diverse research needs and interests

Indexing Efficiency

Upon selecting a corpus, our application initiates a comprehensive indexing process, optimizing data for rapid retrieval and ensuring minimal latency during subsequent queries.

Query Functionality

The application supports two distinct query modes to accommodate varying search preferences:
1. Boolean Retrieval Mode
2. Ranked Retrieval Mode
