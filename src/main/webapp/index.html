Search Engine

Our web application provides efficient search and indexing, allowing users to explore large corpora with ease. Whether using the default corpus or uploading their own, users enjoy quick access to relevant information through a simple interface.

Corpus Selection

Users have the flexibility to choose between our default corpus or upload their own, catering to diverse research needs and interests

Indexing Efficiency

Upon selecting a corpus, our application initiates a comprehensive indexing process, optimizing data for rapid retrieval and ensuring minimal latency during subsequent queries.
